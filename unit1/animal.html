Mantis Shrimp

My favorite animal is the Mantis Shrimp.

Mantis shrimp have a powerful punch and use it to hunt fish, other shrimp, or crabs.

Mantis Shrimp Facts

  • A mantis shrimp punch is 50 mph
  • Their eyes have 16 kinds of color receptors
  • They can break aquarium glass with their punch
  • This is another fact about the mantis shrimp
  • Sometimes they are called pistol shrimp
Common Name Mantis Shrimp
Scientific Name Stomatopds
Habitat Salt water
Lifespan 3-6 years
